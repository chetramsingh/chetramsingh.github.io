Pahadon Se Kya Seekha Maine?

22 April 2026

Nainital ke pahad sirf khoobsurat nahi hain, wo guru bhi hain. Yahan maine sabar seekha.

Jab barish hoti hai to lake bhar jaati hai. Jab dhoop nikalti hai to sab sukh jaata hai. Zindagi bhi aisi hi hai - sukh dukh aate jaate rehte hain.

Pahad hamein sikhate hain ki majboot bane raho, jhuko mat. Par naram bhi raho, jaise mitti barish ko sokh leti hai.
