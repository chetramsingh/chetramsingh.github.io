Namaste Doston! Aaj tourist log boat chalate hain, photo khinchate hain. Par kya aapko pata hai ye jheel ek maa ke aansu se bani hai? Purani Baat Hai: Maa Sati ke pita ne unke pati Shiv ji ka apmaan kiya. Dukh me Sati ne agni me kood ke jaan de di. Shiv ji ka gussa aisa tha ki unhone Sati ka shareer kandhe pe utha liya aur tandav karne lage. Vishnu ji ne Sati ke shareer ke 51 tukde kar diye taaki duniya bach jaye. Jahan-jahan tukde gire, wahan Shakti Peeth bane. Kahani 2: Angrez Ko Nainital Kisne Dikhaya Title: Ek Pahadi Ne Angrez Ko Nainital Bech Diya? Asli Kahani Suno Post: Doston, 1841 ki baat hai. Ek angrez vyapari tha - P. Barron. Cheeni ka vyapar karta tha. Garmi me mar raha tha maidaan me. Ek din usko ek pahadi mila. Naam tha... kisi ko nahi pata. Itihaas ne uska naam mita diya. Pahadi bola - "Sahab, thand chahiye? Mere saath aao." 3 din ghane jungle me chalne ke baad Barron ki aankh phati reh gayi. Neeche hari-makhmal jaisi ghaati, beech me moti jaisi jheel, charon taraf baadal. Barron cheekh pada - "This is a fairy dell!" - Ye to pariyon ka desh hai! Bas wahi se Nainital, angrezon ka "Summer Capital" ban gaya. Mall Road bani, bungalow bane. Par sawal ye hai: Wo pahadi kaun tha? Usne apna swarg angrez ko kyun dikha diya? Paise ke liye? Ya dosti ke liye? Kehte hain wo pahadi baad me bahut roya. Bola - "Maine apni maa ko bech diya." Aaj hum Nainital me rehte hain. Par kabhi sochna - agar wo pahadi angrez ko rasta na dikhata, to shayad Nainital aaj bhi sirf hum pahadiyon ka hota. Tumhara Apna, Chetram Singh Kahani 3: Tiffin Top Ka Bhool Bhulaiya Title: Dorothy's Seat Pe Bhoot Rehta Hai? Tiffin Top Ka Raaz Post: Nainital aaye ho aur Tiffin Top nahi gaye? To kya kiya! Par suno iski asli kahani: Angrezon ke time me ek painter thi - Dorothy Kellet. Ghode pe baith ke yahan painting banane aati thi. Ek din ghode se gir gayi. Yahi mar gayi. Angrezon ne yaad me yahan ek seat banayi - "Dorothy's Seat". Log kehte hain: Poornima ki raat ko yahan ek mem ghode pe baithi dikhti hai Tiffin kholte hi hawa tez chalti hai - Dorothy ko bhook lagti hai Yahan se Nainital ka 360° view dikhta hai - Kyunki Dorothy chahti thi sab uski painting dekhein Mera Tujurba: Main 20 saal se logon ko le jata hun. Ek baar ek ladki akeli photo khincha rahi thi. Achanak boli - "Uncle, mere peeche kaun khada hai?" Maine dekha - koi nahi tha. Par photo me uske peeche dhundli si chhaya thi. Gown pehne hue. Darna mat. Dorothy kisi ko nuksaan nahi pahunchati. Bas akeli hai. Koi usse baat karne nahi aata. Agali baar Tiffin Top jao, to ek extra sandwich le jana. Dorothy's Seat pe rakh dena. Kehna - "Didi, aapke liye." Pahad me sab rehte hain - hum bhi, wo bhi. Tumhara Apna, Chetram Singh
