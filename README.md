Naina Devi Temple
23 April 2026

Nainital ka dil hai Naina Devi Temple. Jheel ke kinare sthit ye pracheen mandir Maa Naina Devi ko samarpit hai. Yahan aakar mann ko bahut shanti milti hai. Subah-subah ghanti ki awaz aur jheel ka nazara - zindagi dhanya ho jaati hai. Har Nainital wale ke liye ye mandir shraddha aur shakti ka kendra hai.
